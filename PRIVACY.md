# LISA Privacy Notice

**Version:** 1.1 local testing build  
**Last updated:** July 2026

## Local-first design

LISA is **local-first** for this testing release. Your communication profiles, caregiver contacts, feedback, and testing checklist are stored on your device only.

## Camera processing

- The front camera is used to detect your face and intentional wink sequences.
- **All camera processing happens on the device.** Video is not uploaded to a server in this build.
- LISA does not record or store video files.

## Accounts and cloud

- **No cloud account** is required or used yet.
- **No caregiver notifications** are sent yet — emergency alerts are simulated locally for testing.
- Future cloud features (profile sync, caregiver alerts, backup) will require **clear consent** before use.

## Data stored on device

This testing build may store locally:

- Communication profiles (My Communication)
- Linked caregivers (Caregiver Linking)
- Custom vocabulary
- Feedback you submit in the Feedback screen
- Testing checklist progress
- Onboarding completion status

## Sharing the app

If you share this testing build with family or friends, they should understand that LISA is an early local testing version, not a finished product.

## Contact

Support contact details will be published in a future release. See **About LISA** in the app for updates.
